Process Cards

We’ve helped thousands of developers get their first job. Over that time we've learned a thing or two about what can hold developers back, as well as what can propel them forward.

These cards are designed to help you develop an effective process for making software. They break down your craft into eight separate activities, each with their own role to play.

If you often find yourself at the end of a long day of programming without achieving as much as you'd like — these cards are for you.
